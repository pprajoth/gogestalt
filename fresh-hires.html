High Performing New Hires


With Gestalt's upskilling, new hires can be immediately productive and can lead to faster digitization of your enterprise.



Keep the excitement of new hires, leverage their innate digital comfort, and still help them hit the ground running early. Talent like this would fast track the digitization of your enterprise and could be critical long term assets of the organization. In addition, they can contribute to significant cost optimization as an alternative to expensive lateral hires or more expensive senior talent.


The distinct benefits of such employees are...

  • New hires will be skilled and immediately productive.
  • Better performance due to holistic upskilling on the digital way of doing business
  • Presence of more effective change agents for digital culture transformation



Look for this option when you need to...

  • Set up a high quality farm for future talent
  • Insource business or technology operations, aided by automation
  • Reduce costs or are facing budget cuts
  • Address a shortage of qualified digital resources
